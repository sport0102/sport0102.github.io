Web2Calendar

A Google Chrome Extension for Seamless Calendar Integration

What is Web2Calendar?

Web2Calendar simplifies the process of adding events to your Google Calendar by:

  • Extracting Information: Analyzing webpage content to identify potential event details.
  • AI-Powered Assistance: Using AI to suggest relevant event information.
  • User Control: Allowing you to review and modify the event details.
  • Seamless Integration: Automatically saving the finalized event to your desired Google Calendar.

How It Works

  1. Browse a webpage with event details.
  2. Activate Web2Calendar.
  3. Review the suggested event details powered by AI.
  4. Edit and confirm the details as needed.
  5. Save the event directly to your Google Calendar.

Why Choose Web2Calendar?

  • AI Intelligence: Quickly identify key event information like dates, times, and locations.
  • User-Friendly: Modify and customize event details before saving.
  • Time-Saving: Avoid manual data entry and enjoy a streamlined workflow.

How to Get Started

  1. Install the Extension: Download Web2Calendar from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Authenticate with Google: Use Google OAuth to securely connect your Google Calendar.
  3. Start Adding Events: Let Click To Calendar handle the details while you focus on what matters.
