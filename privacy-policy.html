Privacy Policy for Click To Google Calendar

At Click To Google Calendar, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information while you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

The extension does not collect or store personal information on external servers. However, to provide its services, the following information may be accessed:

  • Google Calendar Permissions:
    • The extension requests access to your Google Calendar through Google OAuth 2.0. This allows the extension to:
      • Read calendar details to display existing calendars for event creation.
      • Create or edit events based on your input.
  • Webpage Content:

    To identify potential events, the extension temporarily processes content from the webpage you are viewing. This content is processed locally and is not stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data accessed by the extension is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Identifying and extracting event details from webpage content.
  • Creating and saving events to your selected Google Calendar.

We do not collect or use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Processing: All data is processed locally on your device. Event details and webpage content are not stored on external servers.
  • No Persistent Storage: The extension does not store data after it completes the task of adding events to your Google Calendar.
  • Google OAuth: Authentication is conducted securely through Google’s OAuth 2.0 protocol, ensuring your credentials are never exposed to unauthorized parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. Your data remains secure and private, accessible only through your Google account with permissions you control.

5. Your Rights

  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke the extension’s access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google Account settings.
  • Control Your Events: All events are created based on your confirmation, and no automated changes are made without your input.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Updates will be published on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.
